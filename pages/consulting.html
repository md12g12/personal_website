MD Photonic Consulting LTD

I run my own photonic consulting company under the name MD Photonic Consulting LTD. I provide integrated photonic component device design/simulation and wafer scale GDS layouts, as well as advice.

My Background

I learned photonic design during my PhD at the Optoelectronics Research Center in Southampton University, where I was responsible for the complete simulation, design, fabrication and testing of several PIC's. After finishing my PhD I worked as the only photonics engineer at a start up company for two years where I produced numerous device designs, working on novel on-chip lasers and amplifiers. I have used many different software's and fabrication providers, which allows me to work within any existing framework you may have.


If you are in need of some photonic simulation or design help then please email me at md12g12@gmail.com, whether it is for a small discussion or a full design cycle.
